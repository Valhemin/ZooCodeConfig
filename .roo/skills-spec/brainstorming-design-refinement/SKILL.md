---
name: brainstorming-design-refinement
description: Refine rough ideas into a validated design before code.
---

Use when the goal is vague, high-impact, or has multiple reasonable designs.

Workflow:
1. Restate the goal and success criteria.
2. Identify constraints, users, data, systems, and risks.
3. Explore 2-3 approaches when tradeoffs matter.
4. Recommend one approach with rationale.
5. Present the design in readable chunks.
6. Ask only high-impact clarifying questions.
7. Save decisions into the spec pack.

Do not jump to implementation while material design questions remain.
