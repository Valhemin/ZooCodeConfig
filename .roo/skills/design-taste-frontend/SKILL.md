# Design Taste Frontend

Use for frontend/UI implementation and redesigns.

Avoid generic AI UI:
- Same centered hero + three cards pattern.
- Random gradients/glassmorphism.
- Emoji-as-icons unless product tone fits.
- Identical rounded cards everywhere.
- Vague marketing copy.

Prefer:
- Clear information hierarchy.
- Intentional asymmetry and rhythm.
- Specific product context.
- Strong empty/loading/error states.
- Responsive and accessible interactions.
