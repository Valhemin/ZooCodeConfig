# Requirements Quality Checklist

Purpose: validate the quality of the requirements, not the implementation.

## Completeness

- [ ] CHK001 Are all core user goals covered by functional requirements? [Completeness]
- [ ] CHK002 Are empty/loading/error/recovery states specified where relevant? [Gap]

## Clarity

- [ ] CHK003 Are vague words such as “fast”, “secure”, “intuitive”, or “robust” quantified or scoped? [Clarity]
- [ ] CHK004 Are success criteria measurable and technology-agnostic where possible? [Acceptance Criteria]

## Consistency

- [ ] CHK005 Are names for the same concept consistent across spec, UX, data model, and API contract? [Consistency]

## Coverage

- [ ] CHK006 Are non-functional requirements specified for security, privacy, performance, accessibility, and observability when relevant? [Coverage]
- [ ] CHK007 Are edge cases and failure modes defined for every critical workflow? [Coverage]

## Traceability

- [ ] CHK008 Can each major requirement be traced to at least one task or acceptance criterion? [Traceability]
